Food Safety Policy

MB PLC is strongly committed to produce and provide safe, nutritious and high quality milk & milk product that fulfill applicable international, national and mutually agreed food safety and quality standards, regulatory and statutory requirements to the highest satisfaction of its customers. The key measure of its success is exceeding the needs and expectations of the customers.

With strong commitment and participation of the management and employees at all levels, the company trains, creates awareness, implements, maintains and continually improve the integrated food safety and quality management system; ensure allocation and effective utilization of resources; establish effective communication arrangement; prepare and respond to emergency situations and accidents; and ensure the continuing suitability, adequacy and effectiveness of the system.

The policy is communicated, understood, implemented, reviewed, updated and maintained at all levels of the organization.
